The Studio

Pen and Ink is an architecture and interiors practice rooted in the belief that the built environment shapes not just how we move through space, but how we think and feel within it.

Who We Are

Founded in 2012, the studio has grown from a small practice focused on residential projects into a multi-disciplinary atelier working across architecture, interiors, and urbanism. We are based in Chennai, with ongoing work across South India and beyond.

We believe in the value of the particular — in buildings that emerge from their specific site, programme, and culture rather than from generic typologies. Our process is slow and deliberate, and we take on only as many projects as we can give full attention to at any one time.

The name refers to the act of drawing — the foundational discipline that links thinking to making. Every project begins with a line on paper.

Our Values

Restraint

We believe less is almost always more. The discipline of leaving things out is as important as the decision of what to include.

Material Honesty

Materials should do what they claim to do. We avoid surfaces that pretend to be something they are not.

Site Specificity

Every project responds to its exact location — its light, its ground, its neighbours, its climate.
