What Drives Us π

Architecture

We sculpt buildings that breathe with their surroundings — structures that respond to light, climate, culture, and context. Our architecture is timeless, refined, and human-first. We design homes, communities, workplaces, and experiences where people feel grounded yet inspired.

Interior Design

Interiors, to us, are emotional landscapes. We compose atmospheres — through materials that speak, colours that comfort, and furniture that feels like an extension of the user's story. We craft spaces meant to be lived in, loved in, remembered.

What We Create π

Founded in 2025, our practice was built on a shared vision between three seasoned professionals — Karthik, Vandana, and Vijay — who bring together over 18 years of individual experience across architecture, engineering, and design.

With a strong foundation in both technical precision and creative thinking, the studio reflects a balanced approach to the built environment. Karthik, with a background in mechanical engineering, contributes a systems-driven perspective that enhances efficiency, performance, and innovation within every project.

Complementing this, Vandana and Vijay, both trained architects, bring a deep understanding of spatial design, user experience, and contextual sensitivity.

Together, their combined expertise allows the practice to approach each project holistically — ensuring that every space is not just designed, but carefully realised.
